Why full portfolio expansion doesn't happen immediately
Product
Onboarding capacity
Max products per day from one account is unconfirmed. Must ask engineering before committing to any large batch.
→ Confirm with engineering before Amigo July batch
Algorithm
No signal on new products
New products have no booking history. Algorithm recommendations are weaker without it. First batch builds signal.
→ Richer data after 30 days is the expansion proof
Trust
Value must be demonstrated first
Operators commit their full catalog after seeing the algorithm work — not before. Each batch earns the right to the next ask.
→ Day 30 lift data is the unlock
Cost
Full portfolio = significant commitment
Amigo's full portfolio at $250 = $50K/month. That requires demonstrated ROI per product before the big commitment lands.
→ Show ROI per product + portfolio commitment offer
Approval
Multi-team sign-off (Amigo)
Each Amigo city has its own team and approval chain. Full portfolio = multiple simultaneous approvals. Batching by city is the path.
→ Map champion per city · commit city by city
Internal
PA manual setup per product
PA configures every product manually. Large batches queue behind other accounts and slow onboarding for everyone.
→ Agree max batch size with PA before committing
